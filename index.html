What is Henry Stickmin?

A hilarious choose-your-own-adventure game series full of chaotic fails, secret endings, and crazy humor.

When was it made?

The Henry Stickmin Collection was released in 2020 as a remastered version of the series.

Missions

  • Breaking the Bank Henry tries to steal from a secure bank with funny outcomes depending on your choice.
  • Escaping the Prison Henry must escape a guarded prison using different gadgets and wild logic.
  • Stealing the Diamond Help Henry sneak or charge in to grab a priceless diamond—your path shapes the result.
  • Infiltrating the Airship Work with the government or go solo to board a criminal airship filled with traps.
  • Fleeing the Complex Stuck in a snowy fortress, choose allies and plans to break free or fail hilariously.
  • Completing the Mission All paths collide—every decision changes the ending. It’s big, epic, and full of chaos.

Benefits of Playing

  • Endless Fun: Multiple endings keep gameplay fresh and exciting.
  • Humor Galore: Loads of funny fails and Easter eggs to discover.
  • Replay Value: Different choices lead to new surprises each time.
  • Creative Storytelling: Interactive narrative that puts you in control.
